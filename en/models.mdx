### Gemini Omni

`gemini-omni` uses new-api to expose public model names, which can be invoked via `/v1/videos` or `/api/v3/contents/generations/tasks`; use `mode=t2v/r2v/edit` to distinguish text-to-video, reference-based generation, and video editing, and the duration will be automatically mapped to the 4 / 6 / 8 / 10-second tiers.



| Model | Primary Integration Method |

|---|---|

| `gemini-omni` | `/v1/videos` / `/api/v3/contents/generations/tasks` |
